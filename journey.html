THE FOUR CHAPTERS

Progress measured by readiness.

PHASE 01

Building the Foundation

Eliminate consumer debt, strengthen savings, grow flexible income, simplify our lives, launch Operation Wake publicly, and build the financial margin needed to buy the right boat without forcing the decision.

Success Looks Like

Our family is financially and practically ready to purchase a cruising sailboat.

PHASE 02

Becoming Sailors

Purchase our first cruising boat, learn every system, develop safe routines, practice docking and anchoring, build weather judgment, handle maintenance, and discover what living and homeschooling aboard actually require from our family.

Success Looks Like

The boat is trusted, the crew is capable, and daily life afloat feels sustainable rather than temporary.

PHASE 03

Becoming Cruisers

Expand our range gradually through Florida, the Keys, the Dry Tortugas, the Bahamas, and eventually the Caribbean. Learn provisioning, repairs away from home, remote work, passage planning, and how our family functions across weeks and months aboard.

Success Looks Like

Extended cruising has become our normal rhythm, not a vacation from ordinary life.

PHASE 04

Bluewater

Prepare for our first ocean crossing only after years of coastal and island experience, when the family, the vessel, and the conditions are all ready. The crossing is not the beginning of the mission. It is the natural result of everything built before it.

Success Looks Like

We undertake the passage with earned confidence, realistic judgment, and no need to prove anything to anyone.

Every phase prepares us for the next. There are no shortcuts. Only experience earned one voyage at a time.

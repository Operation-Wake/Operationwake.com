OUR STORY

WE DECIDED TO BUILD A DIFFERENT FUTURE.

Operation Wake began with a simple realization: the life we wanted for our family would not arrive by accident.

THE DECISION

Before the voyage, there was a choice.

We are a family preparing for a life shaped by faith, family, freedom, education, and exploration. We are working toward a future where our children can learn from coastlines, weather systems, marine life, navigation, history, and the people we meet.

The boat is part of that future, but it is not the entire mission. Operation Wake is about becoming the family capable of living that life responsibly. That means reducing debt, building flexible income, learning seamanship, strengthening practical skills, and documenting the journey honestly.

THE ROAD AHEAD

We are documenting the build, not just the finish.

PHASE 01

Building the Foundation

Eliminate debt, grow flexible income, simplify our lives, and build the margin needed to buy the right boat without forcing the decision.

PHASE 02

Becoming Sailors

Purchase our first cruising boat, learn her systems, and discover what living and homeschooling aboard actually require from our family.

PHASE 03

Becoming Cruisers

Expand our range gradually — the Keys, the Bahamas, eventually the Caribbean — until extended cruising is our normal rhythm.

PHASE 04

Bluewater

Prepare for our first ocean crossing only once the family, the vessel, and the conditions are truly ready.

MISSION

A LIFE BUILT WITH PURPOSE.

Operation Wake exists to help our family explore, educate, practice stewardship, and inspire others through the honest pursuit of a life on the water.

LET'S BE CLEAR

Operation Wake is about more than sailing.

The boat is simply the vehicle. What we're actually building is a different way to raise a family — one where possessions matter less than experiences, where routine gives way to exploration, and where "someday" turns into an actual plan.

We're not trying to become influencers chasing views, or vacationers escaping responsibility. We're trying to become a capable, prepared family — one that earns its freedom instead of assuming it.

SEVEN FOUNDATIONAL PRINCIPLES

The principles that guide every decision we make.

Everything we build points back to the people doing this together.

VALUE 02 ADVENTURE

A life that includes real risk, real discovery, and real memories.

VALUE 03 EDUCATION

Learning that happens through experience, not just worksheets.

VALUE 04 STEWARDSHIP

Caring for what we're given — our resources, our boat, God's creation.

VALUE 05 FAITH

VALUE 06 INSPIRING FAMILIES

VALUE 07 FINANCIAL DISCIPLINE

WHAT SUCCESS ACTUALLY LOOKS LIKE

The sailboat is a tool. This is the goal.

If, years from now, our children grow into adults who love God, love each other, aren't afraid of hard work, know how to solve problems, care for the world around them, and aren't afraid to chase a meaningful dream of their own — Operation Wake will have succeeded. Even if we never sail around the world.

KNOWLEDGE IS OUR FIRST VOYAGE

Every mile begins with something we can learn today.

Operation Wake is still being built, and so is this library. We are collecting what we learn about boats, seamanship, family cruising, systems, destinations, and the long road from dream to departure. The goal is simple: make the path clearer for the families coming behind us.

FROM THE BEGINNING

We will show the work, not just the horizon.

This library will not pretend we began as experts. It will preserve the questions, decisions, checklists, failures, and breakthroughs that carry Operation Wake from dry land to open water. When experience changes our minds, the library will say so.
